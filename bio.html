Virginia based makeup artist Jasmine Hines has a passion for bringing out confidence with her skill in makeup artistry along with her welcoming personality. Fascinated by all the various features she approaches each makeover as if it was an expedition to find the most favorable attributes on her client’s face. She studies each face carefully, paying close attention to the natural structure, focusing on enhancing and not reconstructing.


Jasmine Hines’ passion for makeup artistry sparked from watching a countless amount of YouTube makeup tutorials. “The transformation is what keeps people interested. Seeing how many illusions I can create while preserving the original face is what fuels my passion for makeup and beauty.”


The year of 2012 has been a great year for Jasmine Hines. Over the summer she had the opportunity to work with her first celebrity client; two VH1 stars from the hit reality show Love and Hip Hop Atlanta. Shortly after working with these well-known ladies Jasmine was selected to be one of nine interns chosen nationally by Crystal Wright—accredited motivational speaker, author, career coach, and educator.

Sitting in Jasmine’s chair quickly shifts from appointment to two girls chatting and playing in makeup. Having the chance to work with Jasmine is the start of a life-long relationship. “I really do care about creating long-term relationships with my clients.” Jasmine’s talent, professionalism, and most importantly connection to her client are what make her stand out from any other artist.



"When it comes to being beautiful, the first step is confidence"
